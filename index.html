To compile the development, simply run:

# coq_makefile -f _CoqProject -o Makefile; make

In the toplevel directory, with coqc in your path. You need at least Coq 8.5beta1 to compile this code, as it makes use of universe polymorphism and fast projections.

The coqdoc documentation is available.
