Functional Extensionality

Propositional functional extensionality is a direct consequence of the definition of equality at product types. It is simply witnessed by a natural transformation between the (dependent) functions, that is a pointwise equality. This corresponds to the introduction of equality on dependent functions in.
Lemma FunExt Γ (A : Typ Γ) (F : TypDep A) (M N : Elt (Prod (LamT F))) (α : Elt (Prod (LamT (Id (↑ M @@ Var A) (↑ N @@ Var A))))) : Elt (Id M N).
where ↑M is the weakening for terms.
